# SLA and Support Plan

## Support Channels
- Primary: GitHub Issues
- Optional: client email/Slack linked to issue IDs

## Severity and Response Targets
- `P1` Critical outage: response within 4 business hours, mitigation within 1 business day.
- `P2` Major feature impairment: response within 1 business day, fix target within 3 business days.
- `P3` Minor bug/UI defect: response within 2 business days, fix in next scheduled release.
- `P4` Enhancement request: triage in backlog review, scheduled by scope and budget.

## Bug Fix Windows
- Hotfix window: same week for P1/P2.
- Standard window: next patch release for P3.

## Enhancement Process
1. Ticket created with business goal and acceptance criteria.
2. Scope + estimate provided.
3. Client approval (time/cost) required before implementation.
4. Delivered via feature branch and tagged release.

## Out of Scope (unless contracted)
- Third-party platform policy changes.
- Custom integrations not documented in SOW.
- 24/7 on-call support.
